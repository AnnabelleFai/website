About Me

I'm Annabelle Fai, a passionate advocate for social justice, a creative thinker, and someone who believes in the power of people to create real change. I'm working to build a better world through community, policy, and hard work.

What I Do

  • Policy research and legislative analysis centered on economic and social equity
  • Direct social work practice supporting individuals and families navigating systemic challenges
  • Advocacy and community engagement, giving voices to the voiceless and demanding positive change

What I Believe

My values are grounded in the belief that every person deserves dignity, safety, and opportunity. I believe in:

  • Centering people, not profits, in public policy
  • Fighting for systems that are inclusive, transparent, and accountable
  • Working alongside communities—not speaking for them
  • Creating change that is practical, sustainable, and rooted in justice
